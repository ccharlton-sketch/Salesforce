Addressing the Fear
We're Not Killing the UI
"You may not even log into Salesforce anymore." — Parker Harris
  • Some users may prefer conversational interfaces over screens — that's a valid choice we now support
  • We are not forcing anyone's hand
  • Active, growing UI roadmap — screen flows, record updates, Lightning improvements continue
  • Some customers want ChatGPT for Salesforce. Some want traditional UI. We're building both.
  • Headless 360 is an option, not a mandate
Bottom line: Headless 360 is a new door — it doesn't close the old one.
